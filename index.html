My Training Philosophy

PLAYER DEVELOPMENT

At the core of GH Trainings are innovative coaching methods that have been revolutionizing player development for years. Philosophies are centered around skill acquisition science, detailed movement training, deep dives into player psychology, and integrating perspectives from basketball cultures all around the world to build highly skilled and adaptable athletes and humans.

Core principles

  • Hard work and effort: Maximum effort is non-negotiable; a strong work ethic is the cornerstone of respect and improvement.
  • Practice makes perfect: Skills are practiced correctly to build good habits, not just playing games.
  • Skill and decision-making: Training connects skills with decision-making in game-like scenarios for effective learning.
  • Game-specific conditioning: Conditioning integrated into gameplay improves skills and endurance simultaneously.
  • Individual and team development: Growth happens individually but within the context of team play.
